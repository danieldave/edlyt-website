The Foundation of Global Textiles

Lexsz Group is the pre-eminent manufacturer and exporter of Polyester Staple Fibre (PSF) and PET Flakes in West Africa. With over 10 years of mastery in polymer production, we deliver supreme quality and unmatched consistency to clients in Europe, the USA, the Middle East, and Asia.

Our commitment to excellence is backed by two state-of-the-art facilities in Nigeria and Ghana. Spanning over 50,000 square meters and employing more than 2,000 dedicated professionals, we guarantee industrial stability for your supply chain.
